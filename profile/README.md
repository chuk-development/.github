## What we build

We build small, focused tools that respect the user. No tracking, no profiling, no closed AI black boxes. Everything we ship is auditable: open-source clients, transparent infrastructure, and open-weight models only.

## Principles

- **Privacy by architecture, not by promise.** Encryption happens on the device. Servers can't read what they don't hold the keys to.
- **Open weights only.** We use DeepSeek, Llama, Mistral, Qwen and other auditable models — never closed-source LLMs.
- **No tracking.** No analytics SDKs, no fingerprinting, no behavioural profiles.
- **One clear price.** Subscriptions cover infrastructure and AI credits. No upsells, no ads, no data brokering.
- **Boring tech, audited code.** Flutter, Supabase, Postgres, standard cryptography. Nothing exotic in the security path.
