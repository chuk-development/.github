## What we build

Small, focused tools that respect the user. No tracking, no profiling, no closed AI black boxes. Everything we ship is auditable: open-source clients, transparent infrastructure, and open-weight models only — operated from Germany under EU law.

## Chuk Chat — flagship

> **Private AI that actually belongs to you.** Chat, write, code, research, and generate images — powered entirely by transparent open-weight models (DeepSeek, Kimi, Qwen, Z.AI, MiniMax, Mistral) via OpenRouter.

<p align="center">
  <a href="https://chat.chuk.chat"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/chuk-development/chuk_chat/master/assets/screenshots/screenshot_home_ui.webp" width="85%" alt="Chuk Chat interface"></a>
</p>

- **End-to-end encrypted** — chats are encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM before they ever leave your device. Zero-knowledge backend: we can't read them, even if the server is compromised.
- **Cross-platform** — one Flutter codebase for Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, Windows, and Web.
- **Real tools, not just text** — web search, live charts, maps, weather cards, image generation, Spotify, calendar & reminders, all rendered inline.
- **One clear price** — a simple monthly subscription covers platform access and AI credits. No token stress, no upsells.

## Principles

- **Privacy by architecture, not by promise.** Encryption happens on the device. Servers can't read what they don't hold the keys to.
- **Open weights only.** DeepSeek, Qwen, Mistral, Kimi and other auditable models — never closed-source LLMs. You always know what's running your AI.
- **No tracking.** No analytics SDKs, no fingerprinting, no behavioural profiles. A tool, not a marketing funnel.
- **One clear price.** Subscriptions cover infrastructure and AI credits. No upsells, no ads, no data brokering.
- **Boring tech, audited code.** Flutter, Supabase, Postgres, standard cryptography. Nothing exotic in the security path.
- **Made in Germany, GDPR by design.** Operated under EU law — your data stays yours: fully deletable, fully controllable.
